Overview

What's a video game?


According to wikipedia, video game is an electronic game that involves interaction with a user interface to generate visual feedback on a video device such as a TV screen or computer monitor. The main question for this project is "what are the gainful genres in video game?" Our project is analysing datas on video game genres like shooter, action, role-playing, sport games, adventure, fighting, strategy, racing and others. For the answer of the question "what are the gainful genres in video game?", we need datasets related to video game genres to analyse.
